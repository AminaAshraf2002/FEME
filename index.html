ABOUT US

Why We Started Feme

We saw the gap. We felt the silence.

We knew that mental health conversations were missing in our communities—and we couldn't ignore it. That's why we started Feme: a platform built on empathy, backed by science, and powered by technology. This isn't just a company for us—this is where we've poured our entire hearts.

We are a community-driven initiative on a mission to:

Spread mental health awareness through accessible psycho-education
Provide world-class therapy through certified mental health professionals
Make therapy inclusive and safe for everyone—regardless of gender, background, or identity

SERVICES

Our Services

Comprehensive mental health support tailored to your unique needs

One-on-One Therapy

Private online sessions with experienced psychologists, counselors, and clinical therapists tailored to your specific mental health needs.

Mental Health Education

Interactive workshops, webinars, and comprehensive resources designed for schools, colleges, and workplaces to promote mental wellness.

Community Support Circles

Facilitated group sessions where you can share experiences, listen to others, and grow together in a supportive environment.

Emergency Counseling

24/7 quick-access support for urgent emotional or psychological distress with trained crisis intervention specialists.

Stress Management

Learn effective techniques and strategies to manage stress, build resilience, and maintain mental wellness in daily life.

Mindfulness Training

Guided mindfulness and meditation practices to enhance self-awareness, reduce anxiety, and improve overall mental well-being.
